Understanding the Complexity of Appliance Removal

Removing an appliance is more than just lifting and loading. Large machines like refrigerators, washers, dryers, and stoves often have electrical or water connections. Disconnecting these without the right knowledge can cause damage or leaks. Some appliances also contain chemicals like refrigerants, which require proper disposal under environmental regulations. Without professional training, appliance removal can be dangerous both for the individual and the environment.
